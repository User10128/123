Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Welcome to Checkers. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us while enjoying our game. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and exactly how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you register an account to play on our platform, we collect the following minimum necessary personal information:

  • Your Name / Username: The display name you choose during registration.
  • Your Email Address: The email address provided during account creation.

2. How We Use Your Information

We believe in strict data minimization. We only use your collected information for the specific purposes outlined below:

Use of Your Name

Your chosen name or username is used exclusively for our competitive leaderboards and in-game matchmaking. It allows other players to identify you on the global rankings and match screens.

Use of Your Email Address

Your email address is kept strictly confidential and is used only for account saving and communication. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Securely save your game progress, win/loss records, and account settings.
  • Facilitate account recovery (e.g., password resets).
  • Send critical, service-related communications (we will never send you marketing spam).

3. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your email address will never be displayed publicly. We implement standard security measures to safeguard your account data against unauthorized access.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or wish to request the deletion of your account and data, please contact us at:

help@checkers.page
