How to Play

Goal: Be the last player standing! Play to the single center pile. If you can't play on your turn, you are eliminated.

The Color Wheel (Adjacency)

Grapefruit ↔ Orange ↔ Lemon ↔ Lime

How to Stack

  • Different Fruit: Play a fruit that is adjacent to the center fruit on the wheel above. (Size does not matter).
  • Same Fruit: Play the exact same fruit, but the physical size of your fruit MUST be larger than the center one!

Special Rules

  • Restocking: If you play your last card, you instantly draw 5 more to keep fighting!
